History:

Uttar Pradesh is famous for handicrafts such as carpet weaving, hand printing, chikan (a type of embroidery), metal enameling, brocade and brass, and ebony work. UP also has the biggest brass and copperware manufacture area in India. The history of the State of Uttar Pradesh is very ancient and interesting.

Speciality

With religious and cultural significance as its identity, Andhra Pradesh is famous for rivers, such as Krishna and Gosthani, hills, such as Horsley and Papi, temples, the Kuchipudi dance form and production of Mica. Also known as the Rice Bowl of India, Andhra Pradesh is the biggest exporter of rice in India.

Location

Uttar Pradesh, the most populous and fourth largest state of India. It lies in the north-central part of the country.
